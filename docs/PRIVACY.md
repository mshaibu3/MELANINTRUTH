# Privacy

Skin and face images are high-risk personal data. Processing requires explicit, purpose-specific, versioned, revocable consent. Images are not used for model improvement unless explicitly opted in. The platform does not infer race or ethnicity and does not sell user data. Export and deletion requests are tenant-scoped and auditable.
